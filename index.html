Career and Interests

The Philippines is a vibrant, diverse archipelago known for its deep-rooted hospitality, resilient spirit, and endless culinary discovery. Often called the “Pearl of the Orient Seas,” it is a treasure trove of intense flavors, bustling street-side markets, and a unique fusion of indigenous, Asian, and colonial traditions. For a food explorer, it is an immersive experience where every province tells a distinct story, offering a harmonious blend of bold, savory, sour, and sweet notes that reflect the richness of its heritage and the warmth of its people.
