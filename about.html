The Heart Behind the Keys

Our journey began with a simple belief: music is a lifelong conversation.

Meet Aaron

Hello! I am Aaron, the founder and lead instructor at Talented Keys. My fascination with music started at a young age, leading me to explore the intricate world of functional harmony—what I like to call 'The Harmonic Architect'.

With over a decade of experience, I've dedicated my life to helping students find their unique voice. Whether we are dissecting a Chopin Nocturne or learning the basics of a major scale, my goal is to blend technical precision with genuine artistry.

Outside the studio, you'll often find me developing tools for music theory or attending classical recitals to stay inspired by the Urtext tradition.
