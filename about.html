The Heart Behind the Keys

Our journey began with a simple belief: music is a lifelong conversation.

Meet Aaron

Hello! I am Aaron, the founder and lead instructor at Talented Keys. My journey in music is rooted in a deep academic foundation from the University of the Philippines College of Music, where I majored in Music Education.

Throughout my studies and professional career, I have mastered various pedagogical methods designed to help students learn holistically. My approach goes beyond just hitting the right notes; I aim to develop the student's ear, heart, and mind simultaneously.

Whether we are dissecting a Chopin Nocturne or exploring the basics of functional harmony through my 'Harmonic Architect' framework, my goal is to blend technical precision with genuine artistry. I am dedicated to helping every pianist find their unique voice in a professional and nurturing environment.

Outside the studio, you'll often find me developing digital tools for music theory or attending classical recitals to stay inspired by the Urtext tradition.
