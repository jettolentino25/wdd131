Site Name

PianoMastery (Talented Keys)

I chose this name because it conveys a sense of achievement and skill-building, which is the core goal for my piano students.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Talented Keys is to provide an online hub for piano students to access structured lessons, track their progress, and find resources for mastering musical theory and performance.

Scenarios

  • "I'm a complete beginner with no musical background. Where should I start my first lesson?"
  • "I want to learn how to play popular songs. Do you have a list of beginner-friendly sheet music?"

Color Schema

The following colors will be used to create a professional and focused learning environment:

  • Primary - Navy Blue (#003366): Used for headers, footers, and primary headings.
  • Accent - Yellow (#FFB81C): Used for call-to-action buttons and important highlights.

Typography

  • Georgia (Serif): Used for all headings to provide a classic, musical feel.
  • Arial (Sans-Serif): Used for body text to ensure high readability on all devices.
